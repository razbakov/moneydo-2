# Experiment: Kakeibo Content SEO

**Requirement:** [Organic User Discovery](../../../governance/requirements/organic-discovery.md)
**Date:** 2026-04-05
**Status:** Proposed

## Hypothesis

We believe that **publishing 5 SEO-optimized articles about the Kakeibo method** will result in **measurable organic traffic growth and at least 10 trial signups from search** for **potential users searching for budgeting and Kakeibo information**.

## Method

1. Growth Lead researches target keywords (Kakeibo-related, budgeting queries) — Week 1
2. Growth Lead drafts 5 articles, Alex reviews and approves — Weeks 2-4
3. Engineer implements any technical SEO fixes identified — Week 2
4. Articles published, one per week — Weeks 4-8
5. Monitor search impressions, rankings, and signups — Weeks 8-16

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | How Measured |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Organic sessions from content pages | >200/month by week 16 | Google Analytics |
| Trial signups from content | >10 in first 3 months | UTM-tracked signups |
| Keyword rankings | 3+ articles on page 1 for target keywords | Google Search Console |

## Pivot Triggers

If any of the following are true at evaluation time, the hypothesis is wrong:

- <50 organic sessions/month from content pages after 16 weeks
- 0 trial signups attributable to content after 3 months
- No improvement in keyword rankings after 12 weeks

## Persevere Criteria

All of the following must be true to continue on this path:

- [ ] At least 100 organic sessions/month from content pages
- [ ] At least 5 trial signups from content in 3 months
- [ ] Positive ranking trend for target keywords

## Timeline

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Content doesn't rank | M | H | Target long-tail keywords with lower competition |
| Traffic but no conversions | M | M | Optimize CTAs and signup flow |
| Kakeibo niche too small | L | H | Expand to broader "mindful budgeting" topics |
