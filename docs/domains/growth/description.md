# Growth — Domain Description (Delegation Canvas)

**Date/Version:** 2026-04-05
**Delegator:** Alex Razbakov (Founder)
**Review date:** 2026-07-05

## Purpose
**Primary Driver:** MoneyDo has no marketing budget and relies entirely on organic discovery. Potential users searching for Kakeibo apps, mindful budgeting tools, or simple expense trackers are unlikely to find MoneyDo because the site lacks SEO-optimized content and educational material.
**Main Requirement:** Attract potential users through content marketing, SEO, and educational material about Kakeibo and mindful budgeting, converting visitors into trial users.

## Key Responsibilities
- Create and optimize content around Kakeibo, mindful budgeting, and personal finance
- SEO optimization of existing and new pages
- Landing page copy and conversion optimization
- Monitor organic traffic and search rankings
- Identify content opportunities from search intent data

## Customers and Deliverables
| Customer | Deliverable |
|----------|-------------|
| Potential users | Educational content, clear product messaging |
| Product domain | User feedback from content engagement, search intent insights |
| Alex (Founder) | Growth reports, content performance data |

## Dependencies
| Provider | What they deliver |
|----------|-------------------|
| Engineering domain | Technical SEO implementation, landing page builds |
| Product domain | Product positioning, feature highlights for content |
| Alex (Founder) | Brand voice approval, content strategy direction |

## External Constraints
- No paid acquisition budget — organic only
- Content must be honest and helpful, not clickbait
- Must respect user privacy (no aggressive tracking or retargeting)
- Brand tone: calm, mindful, practical — not aggressive or salesy

## Key Challenges
- Competing for "kakeibo" and "budget app" keywords against established players
- Creating enough content to build topical authority with limited resources
- Measuring content → trial conversion accurately

## Key Resources
- Nuxt Content module for blog/content pages
- Google Search Console for SEO data
- Existing /kakeibo page as a content seed
- Kakeibo methodology as endless content source

## Delegator Responsibilities
- Approve content strategy and brand voice
- Review content before publication
- Provide product context for content creation

## Competencies, Qualities, and Skills
- SEO knowledge (technical and content)
- Content writing (educational, not promotional)
- Understanding of Kakeibo and personal finance basics
- Analytics interpretation

## Key Metrics and Monitoring

| Metric | How Measured | Frequency | Measured By |
|--------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Organic traffic | Google Analytics / Firebase | Monthly | Growth Lead |
| Search rankings for target keywords | Google Search Console | Monthly | Growth Lead |
| Trial signups from content | Firebase Analytics (UTM tracking) | Monthly | Growth Lead |
| Content output | Pages published per month | Monthly | Growth Lead |

## Evaluation Schedule
Quarterly review by Alex. Assess: traffic growth trend, keyword rankings, content → trial conversion rate.
