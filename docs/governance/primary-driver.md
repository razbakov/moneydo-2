# Primary Driver and Main Requirement

## Primary Driver

People track their spending across scattered methods — mental math, spreadsheets, banking apps, paper notes — without connecting daily expenses to their broader financial goals. This leads to impulsive spending that doesn't align with personal values, failed savings targets, and ongoing anxiety about money decisions. A mindful budgeting approach rooted in the Kakeibo tradition would help people spend intentionally and build lasting financial habits.

---

## Main Requirement

### Intended Outcomes
- Users make daily spending decisions with confidence, knowing how each expense affects their monthly and long-term goals
- Users build consistent savings habits through small daily actions rather than occasional large efforts
- Users reduce spending on things that don't bring them joy or align with their values

### Enabling Conditions
- A simple, accessible tool (web + mobile) that requires minimal effort to use daily
- Clear connection between daily budget, monthly plan, and long-term financial strategy
- Kakeibo's four-category system embedded in the tracking experience
- Subscription model (3€/month) that sustains ongoing development without ads or data selling
