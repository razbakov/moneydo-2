# MoneyDo — Organization Canvas

## Purpose
Help people plan personal budgets and track daily expenses mindfully using the Kakeibo method, so they spend intentionally and achieve their financial goals.

## Overall Strategy
See [strategy.md](strategy.md)

## Customers, Partners, and Stakeholders
- **Primary customers:** Individuals who want to take control of their personal finances — particularly those drawn to mindfulness, minimalism, or the Kakeibo tradition
- **Secondary customers:** Couples or households managing shared budgets
- **Partners:** None currently; potential: financial education content creators, minimalism bloggers
- **Stakeholders:** Alex Razbakov (founder, sole developer), end users whose financial data must be protected

## Business Model(s)
- Freemium SaaS: 30-day free trial, then 3€/month subscription
- Value proposition: simple, mindful budgeting — not feature-bloated financial software
- Revenue = subscriber count × 3€/month
- No ads, no data selling

## Environmental Conditions
- **Legal/Economic:** GDPR compliance required (EU-based users, EU-based founder). Financial data is sensitive — encryption and privacy-by-design are non-negotiable.
- **Market trends:** Growing interest in mindful spending, financial independence (FIRE movement), and simple digital tools. Subscription fatigue is real — the tool must justify its cost monthly.
- **Competition:** Mint, YNAB, Toshl, Bluecoins, and numerous free expense trackers. Differentiation: Kakeibo methodology, radical simplicity, no ads/data selling.
