# Requirement: Organic User Discovery

**Actor:** Potential users
**Domain:** Growth

## Purpose

### Driver
MoneyDo has no marketing budget and relies on organic traffic. Currently, people searching for "kakeibo app," "mindful budgeting," or "simple expense tracker" are unlikely to find MoneyDo because the site lacks SEO-optimized content and educational material about the Kakeibo method.

### Requirement
**Potential users** need to discover MoneyDo through search engines and content so that the product grows without paid acquisition.

## Priority
High

## Type
Operations

## Notes
Content strategy around Kakeibo education, budgeting tips, and mindful money habits. The /kakeibo page exists but needs expansion and SEO optimization.
