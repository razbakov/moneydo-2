# Requirement: Daily Expense Tracking

**Actor:** End users
**Domain:** Product

## Purpose

### Driver
Users who plan budgets but don't track actual spending lose awareness of where money goes. The gap between planned and actual spending grows invisibly until it's too late to course-correct within the month.

### Requirement
**End users** need a frictionless way to log expenses as they happen so that they can see how today's spending affects their remaining daily and monthly budget in real time.

## Priority
High

## Type
Operations

## Notes
Expense logging exists but needs to stay fast (under 10 seconds to log an expense). Kakeibo's four categories (needs, wants, culture, unexpected) should guide categorization.
