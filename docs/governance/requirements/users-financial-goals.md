# Requirement: Long-term Financial Goals

**Actor:** End users
**Domain:** Product

## Purpose

### Driver
Users who track daily expenses without connecting them to larger goals treat budgeting as bookkeeping rather than a tool for achieving what matters to them. They may save sporadically but lack a strategy for debt payoff, large purchases, or vacation funds.

### Requirement
**End users** need to set and track long-term savings goals so that daily budgeting feels purposeful and progress toward meaningful financial milestones is visible.

## Priority
Medium

## Type
Operations

## Notes
This connects short-term (daily budget) to long-term (goals). Could be as simple as named savings targets with progress bars. Kakeibo emphasizes reflecting on what brings joy — goals should connect to that.
