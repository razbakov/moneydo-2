# MoneyDo — Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Purpose
**Driver:** See [primary-driver.md](primary-driver.md)
**Requirement:** Help users make mindful daily spending decisions connected to their long-term financial goals, through a simple Kakeibo-based tool.

## Intended Outcome(s)
- A reliable, simple web app that users open daily to check their budget and log expenses
- Steady subscriber growth through organic discovery (SEO, word-of-mouth) rather than paid acquisition
- A sustainable solo operation augmented by AI agents for product, engineering, and growth work

## Strategy
Build a focused, Kakeibo-first budgeting tool that wins on simplicity rather than features. While competitors add complexity (investment tracking, bill splitting, bank integrations), MoneyDo stays radically simple: plan your budget, see your daily allowance, track what you spend, reflect on what brings value.

Growth comes from content marketing around Kakeibo and mindful money habits — attracting people who search for "kakeibo app" or "mindful budgeting." The subscription price stays low (3€/month) to reduce churn and make the decision trivial.

AI agents handle product management, engineering, and growth — allowing Alex to focus on strategic decisions and quality control rather than execution of every task.
